Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CBE (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email, phone number, billing details, uploaded payment proofs.
  • Account Credentials: Username, password (securely hashed), subscription status.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser, device details, page views, session duration.
  • Communications: Messages through contact forms or support channels.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Cookies, localStorage, and similar technologies to personalize your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process applications, payments, and manage VIP access.
  • Support: Respond to inquiries and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improvement: Analyze usage to enhance features and performance.
  • Marketing: Send transactional messages, updates, and promotions (with consent).
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, enforce Terms of Service.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, hosting, email delivery.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law.
  • Business Transfers: Mergers or acquisitions with safeguards in place.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and localStorage to maintain sessions, remember preferences, and collect analytics. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, but some features may be affected.

6. Third-Party Services

We integrate tools such as Supabase, Paystack, and Google Analytics. Please review their privacy policies for details on their practices.

7. Data Security

We implement encryption in transit (HTTPS), secure password hashing, access controls, and regular audits to protect your data.

8. Data Retention

  • Account & Payments: Up to 7 years for compliance.
  • Analytics Logs: Up to 24 months, then aggregated or anonymized.
  • Support Communications: Up to 3 years unless you request earlier deletion.

9. Your Privacy Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, email support@apexincomeoption. We respond within 30 days.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If discovered, we will promptly delete such information.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in Nigeria or by our service providers abroad. We apply appropriate safeguards for any transfers.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the changes. Significant updates will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on our site.
