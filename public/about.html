My Journey

I was born and raised in Ede-Oballa Nsukka, Enugu, Nigeria—a village where tradition and community shaped my soul. As a boy, I learned discipline and perseverance, yet I feared forex trading, seeing it as a gamble that destroyed lives. Life, however, had other plans. Through ups and downs, I built resilience, and when a friend introduced me to gold trading, I took a leap of faith. Late nights with flickering charts brought early wins—and crushing losses. But I refused to quit. Tears of frustration turned into lessons of mastery as I devoured books like Trading in the Zone by Mark Douglas, Currency Trading and Intermarket Analysis by Ashraf Laidi, and Technical Analysis of the Financial Markets by John J. Murphy. Each page taught me to master my mind, decode markets, and embrace discipline over fear.

Today, I stand as proof that forex is not a gamble—it’s a craft. My mission with CBE is to empower others, stripping away the noise to reveal a path to wealth for all who dare to learn and persist.
